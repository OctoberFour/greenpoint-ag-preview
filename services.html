Frequently Asked Questions

Explore common questions we frequently answer to help you understand our mission and goal.

When is the best time to schedule a soil test?
We recommend soil sampling in the fall after harvest. Results come back in 7–10 days, leaving plenty of time for nutrient planning before spring.
How often should I consult with an agronomist?
At minimum, twice a year — planning and mid-season — though active growers often check in weekly during scouting season.
Which precision ag tools do you support?
AccuField, Climate FieldView, John Deere Operations Center, and most other major platforms — we’ll work with whatever your operation already runs on.
Do you offer custom blending and delivery?
Yes — high‑speed blending and 24‑hour automated loadout are available at most retail locations.
